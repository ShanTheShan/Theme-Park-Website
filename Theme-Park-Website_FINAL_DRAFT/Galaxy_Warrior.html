Galaxy Warrior

Embark on an action-packed journey to defend our galaxy from the evil alien horde! Engage in this stunning triple duelling roller coasters! Modelled after the award winning movie The Galaxy Warriors Strikes Back, this thirlling, spectacular, adrenaline rushing coaster offers users the ultimate roller coaster experience. From twist and turns, to sudden drops, we got it all.



Details and Requirements

  • Minimum Ride Height: 1.65m
  • Maximum Ride Height: 2.05m
  • Minimum Age: 8 years of age
  • Length of Track: 3km
  • Height at Peak: 35m


Due to the nature of the ride, patrons are expected to be physically fit with no underlying health conditions which could compromise their well-being during the duraion of the ride.
