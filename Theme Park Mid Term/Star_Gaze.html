Star Gaze

Prepare to be enchanted by the beauty of our solar system! This smooth,relaxing, slow ride takes you through a magical and educating experience, narrated by critically acclaimed astronaut, Bill Cosby!

  • No Minimum Height Requirement!
  • No Maximum Height Requirement!
  • Minimum Age: 4 years of age
  • Length of Track: 1km

An exrtemely small percentage of patrons may experience a seizure when exposed to certain visual images, including flashing lights or patterns that may appear during the ride. Patrons with a history of seizures are highly advised not to participate in the ride.
